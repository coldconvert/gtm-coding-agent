# Content Drop Template

A "content drop" is a single piece of insight packaged for social distribution. Think of it as the atomic unit of content marketing.

---

## The Drop

### Core Insight
[One sentence. The thing that would make someone stop scrolling.]

### Evidence
[Data point, personal experience, or example that backs it up.]

### Context
[Why does this matter right now? What's the trigger?]

### So What
[What should the reader do with this information?]

---

## Distribution Plan

### LinkedIn Post
[Adapt the drop for LinkedIn format. Hook first line. Short paragraphs. No links in body.]

### Email Mention
[2-3 sentences to include in a newsletter or outbound email.]

### Tweet
[280 characters. Sharpest possible version.]

### Blog Seed
[Could this become a full blog post? What sections would you expand?]
