- [ ] Target keyword identified

## Metadata
- **Title:** [SEO-friendly, under 60 characters]
- **Target keyword:** [Primary keyword]
- **Meta description:** [Under 160 characters, includes keyword]
- **Audience:** [Who is this for?]
- **Goal:** [What should the reader do after reading?]

## Structure

### Hook (1 paragraph)
[Open with a surprising fact, contrarian take, or specific result. No "In today's world..." openings.]

### The Problem (1-2 paragraphs)
[What pain point does the reader have? Be specific. Use their language.]

### The Insight (2-3 sections with H2 headers)
[Your main content. Each section = one idea with evidence. Use code blocks, screenshots, or examples where helpful.]

#### [Section 1 Header]
[Point + evidence + example]

#### [Section 2 Header]
[Point + evidence + example]

#### [Section 3 Header]
[Point + evidence + example]

### The Takeaway (1 paragraph)
[One clear action the reader can take. Specific, not generic.]
