# Buying Signals

## High-Intent Signals
| Signal | Source | Action |
|--------|--------|--------|
| [e.g., Visited pricing page 3x] | Website analytics | Fast-track to sales |
| [e.g., Downloaded comparison guide] | CRM | Send competitive positioning |
| [e.g., Replied to outbound] | Sequencing tool | Personalized follow-up |

## Trigger Events
| Event | Where to Monitor | Response Playbook |
|-------|-----------------|-------------------|
| [e.g., New VP Sales hired] | LinkedIn / job boards | Outbound within 30 days |
| [e.g., Series B announcement] | Crunchbase / news | Budget available, reach out |
| [e.g., Competitor price increase] | G2 reviews / social | Competitive positioning |
| [e.g., Company expansion to new market] | Press / LinkedIn | Timing play |

## Negative Signals (Disqualify / Deprioritize)
| Signal | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| [e.g., Recently signed competitor contract] | Wait 10 months, nurture |
| [e.g., Layoffs in target department] | Budget frozen, deprioritize |
| [e.g., No tech stack overlap] | Poor fit, disqualify |

## Signal Sources
- **First-party:** Website analytics, email engagement, product usage
- **Third-party:** Intent data providers (Bombora, G2), job postings, news
- **Manual:** LinkedIn activity, conference attendance, community engagement
